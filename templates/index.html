How ThreatLens works

ThreatLens combines Google Safe Browsing with simple rule-based checks to give a clear risk score and short educational tips.[web:120]

  • Checks the URL against known malware and phishing lists.[web:120]
  • Looks for suspicious patterns like strange domains or scam keywords.[web:145]
  • Shows a Low / High risk label along with an explanation in simple language.

Goal: not just to flag risky links, but to help users understand why they might be dangerous.[web:152]
